Focus Pad (FP) and FV

  • I have tried migrating to FV with Focus Pad (iPhone App originally released for Auto Focus in 2009) with great success.

    There is a simple list in which you can colour the tasks blue (as an equivalent to the dotted tasks), red (which I use to indicate the current taks - so I can easily find what I am currentrly working on) grey for abandoned.

    Items can also be maked as done, marked as done and automatically re-entered at the end of the list or scheduled for some future date.

    Completed pages can be arcihived and you can access curent pages, archived pages or all pages.

    The whole thing syncs to focuspad.ws so apart from #tags is just about perfect and available anywhere.

    Woth a look if you want a simple electronic version.
