My first (and only!) FV tweak

  • Hi,

    I'm not a tweaker-- really I'm not! But I am starting to use one in FV, and since Mark has noted he has probably tried (and did not incorporate) many variations to the system, I wonder if he had tried this one, and curious to hear others thoughts as well.

    I am marking (using outlook) tasks that are "in progress." They do not affect the selection process, but when I ask myself "The Question" I find it useful to know quickly if I had started something already or not. With 626 items on the main FV list (yes it is growing not shrinking, but I am confident I am getting everything collected and the right things done) it does help to have this little cheat in place.

    For anyone using outlook and curious how I am doing this, in my master FV view I made a conditional formatting rule to slightly enlarge any task that has a %complete greater than 0%. I don't measure % complete it is either at 0 or greater, and those that are greater now pop a little on my list.
