Notetaking with cintanotes

  • Hi,
    This is not ad! ;-)

    Due to a more or less sleepless night I searched the web for an easy but efficient note taking app.
    What I found was cintanotes (http://cintanotes.com/). I run my privatetask on paper, but cintanotes has a good changes to substitue my worklist - I will try this after my vacation ;-)

    What stands out IMO:
    - plain list sorted by entering date (important for AF)
    - tagging (great for assigning projects and/or merging AF with GTD if you want)
    - great search feature

    Worth a look!
    Take care,
    Jens
