    It is a very easy system. Any one can do it with paper and a 4 color pen.

    I indeed thanks everyone for your ideas. I did not invent anything. In only took what I thought some best ideas and did one method around it.

    You will need
    a paper notebook
    a 4 color pencil
    Your attention and your brain

    You will need to choose one color per day.
    We are thursday the chosen color is blue.

    Making & starting
    1. Do one long list of everything which comes to your mind in blue (today's color)
    2. Write everything one after the other it can be ideas, projects, someday may be

    What if ?
    - If you plan to do it tomorrow, or if it is something new coming today which can wait, write it in red, it will be your tommorow list.

    - If something must be done or could be done later than tomorrow (we, monday....) write it in another color letâ€™s say for the we green and set a date at the end of the task (ie 06/18)

    - If you dont know what day to do it, write it in blue (the dayâ€™s color) the system will sort it anyway...

    - If something is indeed important or so urgent highline it in yellow and do it asap or follow it...

    So your list is complete, and your system operational.

    Acting
    use woobaâ€™s system. For memory :

    "Write a list. Everything goes on the list (but see notes below). Work the list in order. You cannot skip an item, but you have options for each item:

    1. Cross it off
    2. Do it and cross it off
    3. Do some of it, cross it off and rewrite at the end of the list
    4. Schedule it on calendar and cross it off
    5. Donâ€™t do it, but cross it off, split it into two new tasks and write them at the end of the list"

    Except reading the list jump on colors that matter (avoid the red it is for tomorrow) avoid the green it is for later)

    Begin by the back log (ie black for monday and tuesday, blue for wednesday and thursday.

    Tomorrow morning begin with the highlined tasks (the most imprtant or urgent) and the red one (your wednesday list) dont do others tasks before having done the red one or the highlined. If you have finished go back to the back log and do it one by one...
