DWM - Using 2 pages per day?

  • I wonder of there is any advantage in using a 2 pages per day diary.

    One one page could be listed all the immoveable activities for the day or scheduled items.

    On the other pages could be listed all the DWM items.

    This means having everything in one place and planning days with more information to hand. I'm not very good at doing any of these things so thought it could be an advantage. However I may have overlooked some fatal flaw in my thinking!

    Has anyone got any thoughts on this?
