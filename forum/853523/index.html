Need a way to scan the list

  • I'm in a particularly hectic period--things are happening very fast on many fronts. I'm loving the new revised AutoFocus. But I find that now and then I get a little anxious over what might be lurking within my list that may be becoming urgent. So I find myself scanning the list to see if there's something, and if I find one I go ahead and do it "out of order." I'm wondering if I am undermining something by doing this. Perhaps I should add a task: Scan list for urgent items? Or am I missing something that would handle this?

  • Thanks for the suggestions. I suppose the way one could handle this without modifying the system would be to consider the feeling of anxiety that there is something urgent lurking somewhere in the list as a sign that no task is ready to be acted upon until the task that is causing the anxiety is discovered and acted upon or until the feeling goes away. In that way, a full scan can happen at any point within Reverse Mode or Forward Mode.

    I still have a feeling that I'm missing something, but at least this approach satisfies my need for assurance that nothing is getting neglected. I missed the first-thing-in-the-morning scan initially, but really appreciated the "permission" to get right into urgent tasks during this period of urgent tasks proliferating much faster than is usual for me.
