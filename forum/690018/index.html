Post-it notes hack to highlight "must do today" items

  • I used to follow the DIT methodology and particularly liked the aspect of
    knowing what things had to be completed on that day. But I got tired
    of rewriting items on the next day (AF solves that problem!)

    Now that I am using AF, I identify the tasks that need to be done today
    with a "." to the left. Sometimes these items are not immediately obvious
    particularly when scattered across several pages, so I created
    a hack.

    I have a small number of post-it notes about 3 x 2cm in size with
    an arrow drawn on the sticky side. I then place these post-it notes
    against the item. The note does not protrude outside the pages
    but it means I can quickly see the "Must-Do" items today.
    As I complete the items I can store the postit notes somewhere
    else in my book for later usage.

    I used this technique to remind me of 3 things I had to do
    in the evening.

    Another possibility is using different coloured Post-It notes for color-coding, eg
    Pink for phone calls, Green for emails to send.

    I read a productivity tip somewhere of asking yourself
    each morning "What are the 2 or 3 most important things I can do
    today for for professional and personal life?". These 2 or 3 items
    could be tagged with post-it notes.

    And another important hack. Write your name, email address and
    phone number on your AF notebook. It would be most incovenient to
    lose it!
