How do you *start* using AF correctly?

  • Folks,

    I just want to make sure I understand Mr. Forster's guide for starting AF (i.e. the new and improved AF1+2).

    1. Don't put a tremendous number of tasks on the list to begin with. Don't front load it! Things will be added as you go along.

    2. You don't draw and lines across the page on DAY1, do you?

    3. At the beginning of DAY2, you draw a line after the first contiguous block of unactioned items. These are "on notice" for review, which would happen on DAY3.

    4. NO items would be on review on DAY2 (i.e. you wouldn't highlight any)

    5. At the beginning of DAY3 you would put on review (highlight) all unactioned items above the line. You would then draw a new line after the first contiguous block of unactioned items.

    Does this look correct? Any additions or clarifications?

    Thanks!
