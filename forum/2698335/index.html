Steps on how to do HITM

  • Stay stupid. Be compulsive. Have faith.

    1) Any smart person would realize the length and arduousness of your list and would not even touch it. A dumb person would not only think about how long and hard it is but would be cocky enough to believe he can do the list anyway. Ignorance and arrogance are your friends. Do not think, never brood: dot it, then do it.

    2) Not only should you not think in choosing and doing tasks, but even in stopping. The moment you feel you have to stop, do not think about it: stop! Then choose, then do.

    3) Believe in the righteousness of what you are doing. Believe in that something that we cannot see, nor hear, nor taste, nor even sometimes feel that is doing the "standing out" for you. It is not magic, and thus it does need the data that you give it, but intuition is real. Believe in it.

    And yes this was inspired by "Do the Work" by Steven Pressfield.

    http://www.amazon.com/Do-Work-Steven-Pressfield-ebook/dp/B00NK0MJBK

    Pressfield believes in the existence of Resistance. I do too. But I think HITM has a great approach on how to deal with it: outrun it.
