Sunday funtime: a new dice-rolling app

  • Dr. Drang's blog has a great post on a new dice-rolling app from the developer of the popular PCalc app for iOS. For those of you still using dice to randomize your task selection, it looks like a cool addition to your arsenal.

    The fun part, for those of you who interpret it as fun, is that Dr. Drang walks you through all the math, 1000 random throws, Python scripting, and chi-square and degrees of freedom goodness to determine that the app does indeed throw digital dice as randomly as you would real dice.
