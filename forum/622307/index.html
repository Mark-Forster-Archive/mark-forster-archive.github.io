    5 January 2009 at 20:07 | Frank
  • I use Outlook exclusively and am very used to being able to create tasks from e-mails as well as have plenty of space to store information within each task. I am using categories to handle my pages for Autofocus. I just named the first page "~ Pg 1" and placed 30 objects into that page, then created "~ Pg 2" etc. What I really like is then you can just view your task list by category and expand or collapse pages as needed. It is also easy to transfer items between pages by just dragging and dropping. I already have the view formatted to strike through completed objects and all I need to do is create another category for dismissed items and format it so that it will change the font color to simulate a highlighter. I am also a long time Palm user and I use Beyond Contacts by Data Viz to get around the 15 category limit in the typical Palm software, so if I ever have to go beyond 15 pages there will be space to do it.

    Thanks Mark for a wonderful system that really feels comfortable. I can't wait to see how it performs over time.
