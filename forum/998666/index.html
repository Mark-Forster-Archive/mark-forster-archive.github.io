    http://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The_Science_of_Getting_Rich

    there are plenty of people trying to sell it, but this version is free. I have no guarantee if will work or if rubbish, but at least it is not expensive.

    Same goes for generic modern motivation, can find good information all over the web. And of course planning and getting things done from this site.

    If you combine:

    1. a sort of where do I want to go with my life and how do I want to live it?
    (do I go from New York to Boston or Providence) (do I take a car or train or bus)

    2. make a plan. (today I buy tickets, tomorrow I pack food for the car trip, friday I drive 50 miles and then stop to see the seashore)

    3. add some of the motivational methods on top, so that things start going your way and you see what is working in your life, solutions come to you and such

    4. then use AutoFocus to make sure your life actually unfolds as you planned

    5. adjust as needed (hey, nice weather, maybe we stay at the beach an extra day)

    this is super short summary, but should get you out of many of your troubles and back on the road again
