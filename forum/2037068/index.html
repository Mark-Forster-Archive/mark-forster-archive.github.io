One great tip which works with mainly all systems

  • Hello

    I love MF systems and especially use (AF), (AF4) and FV. I have discovered a way to go a head when I am stuck on a task. It often happen. If I am reluctant to do something I simply report it at the end of the list and put a cross after the item ie X.

    More there are XXX more the item is urgent or important. So after 2 XX I stop and block a period of time to do something on it whatever...

    Then if something is left I reformulate the item and put it at the end of the list

    The result since 6 month is great. I made many good things with this tip and wanted to share it with you.
