  • If want something simple (and your documents are relatively simple), check out Simplenote. Your docs are stored online, and available from many mobile/desktop clients.

    For a little more structure & features (but not too much), try Evernote. It has fancier notes, tagging, and can recognize text within images (whiteboard, etc). Plus they're an awesome company (no relation other than I'm a customer).

    Both Simplenote and Evernote are free, although Evernote provides premium features for $5/month (well worth it for PDF searching, all file types, and better mobile/offline viewing).
