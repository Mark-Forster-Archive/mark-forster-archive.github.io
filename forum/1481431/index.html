  • moises,

    I may be wrong, but I believe what you are doing with the "x" is task deletion, not dismissal, and is within the official rules for SF. Personally I append "NOT" to the task description before crossing it out instead of putting an X in front, to remind me that I chose to delete it.

    Dismissal is for tasks remaining, that you didn't take action on or delete. Unlike deletion, dismissal is to be done for all remaining (C1) tasks, when you visit the page and find nothing you want to do.
