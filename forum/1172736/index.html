My virtual vacation

  • Today I was scheduled to take time off for vacation. However, circumstances at work mean I can't leave now. (I'm the owner, and it's important to me.) Actually, just preparing and planning to take off was proving to be stressful and now that I'm not taking off, that stress is gone.

    Nevertheless I'm declaring myself to be virtually on vacation. Therefore aside from going to work every day, I'm considering all my ordinary obligations at home to be completely non-pressing. I think in fact though I will still do things around the house, but without feeling any pressure to either do them or not do them, I shall be able to relax.

    It's quite nice! :-)

    Hopefully things go well at work and I can take that real vacation soon. We'll see.
