Going back to the original Autofocus

  • Hi Mark and All,

    I have been quiet for some time now as have been extraordinarily busy. I still am, but I have had an epihany of sorts as of late last night. I agree with Mark -- the versions of AF after the original just have not had the same effect on me. I am not as excited to use them. Another major issue is that I have been finding myself spending too much time on the urgent and not the important. So many urgencies that come my way are other people's urgencies and not mine. Most really can wait.

    So, I have decided to go back to the orginal AF and regain the excitement I had with what I consider still a brilliant and captivating system. As for tasks that are truly urgent and need to be done today -- I have a category in Outlook called "red zone actions" and that is at the top of the list. My AF list will stay in my simple, spiral-bound notebook. Plus, I just use my common sense -- as Mark has always said -- if it HAS to be done now -- I just do it!

    When Mark's new system comes out -- and what he is reporting now, it sounds fantastic -- I am sure I will switch over and give it a try. But for now -- for me -- it is back to the original Autofocus.

    Best wishes,
    -David
