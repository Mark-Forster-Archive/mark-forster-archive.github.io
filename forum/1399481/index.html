AF1 - Moving to the next page and dismissing items?

  • Hi all!

    I'm quite confused about when you're supposed to dismiss items from your list.
    It seems to me, that you're supposed to dismiss all item on a page, if you don't work on any item from it. Or is it, that you have to dismiss only if this happens on the _first_ pass?

    Thanks!
    9 February 2011 at 12:26 | Greg
  • Greg:

    Rule 7 of the AF1 instructions covers this:

    "If you go to a page and no item stands out for you on your first pass through it, then all the outstanding items on that page are dismissed without re-entering them. (N.B. This does not apply to the final page, on which you are still writing items). Use a highlighter to mark dismissed items."

    This means the first pass on that particular visit. What it boils down to is that whenever you visit a page you have to do at least one task on it. Otherwise all the remaining tasks on the page get dismissed.
