Digital system with Omnifocus

  • I tried something I never really did before. Reading my FVP list I realized that most of the items belongs to projects in a GTD way. So I decided today to make it simple and try AGAIN the digital way as I have Omnifocus everywhere and on all my devices.

    So, my Project are organized on actionable and non actionable ie Waiting for
    My Contexts are : "Now list", "SMB (someday may be)", "WF (waiting for)"
    Inbox is used for capturing using a short cut (ALT +N) tasks as they come, if they are'nt relative to a project... If they do they goes into the project.

    FOR WORKING the idea was to put nothing in my paper notebook but notes.

    So, I just took my projects one by one and jot ideas, as they came, then put at the end on my now list what I intend to do asap. I also put a end date on what must be done or if they were incoming at a certain date. I also put on WF what I wait from others or was delagated.

    Result : At the end of the day I did all about I wanted on 4 projects I was reluctant !

    One was the taxes (end date tomorrow)- I was bloqued on that one since a month.
    One was a personnal project, 2 were commercial projects I must close on wednesday.

    The day was not worst than another. It is 18:00 Pm and I have done what I wanted. All my active projects are in order. I will capture new tasks later. Everything is under control.

    You will say this is not MF system. Right ! except that for doing things I used the stand out system letting my intuition choosing what I wanted to do just now. Without planning anything. Just be mentaly beeing present and focus on the now instant.

    Tonight I feel quiet. I have the felling that I done my best.

    When I put my stuff in digital, which for me can be omnifocus or evernote, I loose this feeling sensation. I can find everything but I dont "feel" things. I loose my senses of controlling things, and loose a part of intuition

    With paper I never missed a deadline, it works everytime, everywhere, I spend my ife doing thing instead of organizing thing like I do in OF
