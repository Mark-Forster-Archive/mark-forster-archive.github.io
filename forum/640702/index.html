  • Yes, the Autofocus beta testing is still open to new subscribers. All you have to do is enter your email address in the Newsletter sign-up box in the right hand margin.

    What happens then depends on whether you are already a subscriber to my newsletter:

    If you are not, then you will see a couple of tick boxes, one for my normal newsletter and one for the Beta Testing. Make sure you tick the Beta Testing one and submit. You can of course tick both if you wish.

    If you are, then you will see a message saying you will receive an email with instructions on how to change your sign up details. Press Submit and follow the instructions in the email when you receive it.
