  • I do something quite similar on an A4 sized piece of paper folded into three (so each page is a third of a side of A4)

    At the start of each day and during the day I list items as follows

    - Items I need/want to do today go at the top of the page.
    - Items that can optionally be done today go in the middle.
    - Distractions/grass catcher items go at the bottom.

    When I've completed enough of the need/want to do items I can go back and look at a previous day's items.

    If I'm taking a break I can look back and pick a distraction activity :)

    I was going to post about it on the 'Hybrid AF list...' thread but it isn't really AF.
