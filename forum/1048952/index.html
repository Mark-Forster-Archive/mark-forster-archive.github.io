Read it Swap it

  • I often use this site (no affiliation):

    www.readitswapit.co.uk

    I have bought a lot of books over the years - some I no longer want. For the cost of postage you can swap with other members for books you may want. The concept is very easy and to set up a list of books you want to sell, you only have to enter the ISPN number from the back of the book.

    I notice there are a couple of copies of Mark's books for those of you who may not have a copy or can't afford to buy one. To make sure people want to swap with you it is a good idea to list a number of books in a cross section of interests so they have plenty to choose from.

    You or they can decline a swap if you don't fancy anything on their list of books.

    I like the idea that I can be more efficient in managing my book collection using this website.

    I'm afraid I don't know if they run them outside the UK - I am guessing people won't be keen to swap outside the UK due to the cost of postage of the books.
