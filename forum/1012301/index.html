Monthly Pocket Diary for DWM?

  • I've started on DWM, using 28 index cards because I want something I can write on (nonelectronic) and keep in my pocket. I write small, and can get 3 or 4 columns of 10 rows on each card, which will probably be enough for most days. The unruled backs of the cards are also available.

    It occurred to me that carrying two thin monthly page-a-day pocket diaries might be a much better way to go. Do such things exist? I bought a few Field Notes (fieldnotesbrand.com) booklets, which worked nicely for AF4, and which would work with hand-entered dates for DWM, although they are 48 pages each. And using up a page each day would be a bit pricy - US $25 to $40 for the year, depending on whether I choose to "waste" the unused pages at the end of each month or just consider each booklet to be 48 days.

    What does everyone think?
