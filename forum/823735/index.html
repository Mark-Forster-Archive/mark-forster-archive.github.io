  • I'm still using AF1 because I find AF2's dismissal rules somewhat awkward. And, well, because AF1 works for me.

    Mark, you wrote that the hardest part for AF2 was to figure out how to handle dismissing. Did you make any experiments without dismissal rules? As far as I can see, dismissing is essential in AF1, otherwise the list would grow and grow. With dismissing I regain focus. AF2, however, has another focus: by working backwards earlier items become more and more distant. Eventually, while they are still on the list, technically, they are clearly no longer in focus.
