Jumping straight to a task that I want to do now

  • One thing about AF2 that I'm struggling with a bit is the idea of working your way methodically down the list even if you know you have items further down you really want to do right now.

    For example, just a few minutes ago, I remembered one of the tasks that was "on notice" today. Then I realized that right now would be a great opportunity to do it, so I just did it and it's now off my list.

    I didn't methodically work my way down the list (from the newest to the oldest) looking for "stand out" items. It just felt like the right time so I did it.

    I'm taking the view here that it's a judgement call in situations like this and my ultimate goal anyway was to get myself to do that task.

    So I'm presuming it is okay to have an intuition-based "trump card" that you can play to override the system when it feels necessary?
