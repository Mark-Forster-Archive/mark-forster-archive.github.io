  • Personally, I don't make resolutions or set goals (well, at least not for the year). That leads to fantasizing and makes you feel good without having done anything. It also doesn't really push you, because not keeping your new year resolutions is kind of excepted.

    Instead, I made a *prediction* what will happen this year. A prediction is much more rooted in my current situation (even if it may be well over the top) and I find it much easier to assess at the end of the year and much more illumination. Also I find it strangely exciting.

  • I like to start with DREAMS which can be whatever you like. From them stem GOALS. Of course, not every dream qualifies to become a goal. A goal should be achievable and realistic and should have a target date. However, it is good for goals to be ambitious and challenging. I hope this helps.
