  • Alison:

    << How about things like 'Check on feedback from X' when you don't know when this will come - it's a reminder really - but without a specific date. >>

    You can put those on your list as normal tasks. I usually put a query after them, e.g. "X replied?" They serve as a reminder when you scan the list, and when the person replies you can cross the task off. If they haven't replied after a suitable interval, you can then chase them.
