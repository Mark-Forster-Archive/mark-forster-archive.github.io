Captcha - latest situation?

  • As most of you know the Captcha for the Comments and Forums is inactive because Squarespace is not still maintaining this version.

    As a few people have recently reported that they can't post at all as a result, I want to check on people's latest experiences with the Captcha (or lack of it). Only about half the people posting are signed in, so a lot must be getting through somehow.

    If there's a serious problem (as opposed to a temporary glitch) I'll re-open registration for new members - who get a password which will bypass the captcha altogether. I'm reluctant to do this unnecessarily as it requires quite a bit of work on my part.

    So those who have recently posted a comment without signing in, please let me know how it went - whether you posted successfully or not.
