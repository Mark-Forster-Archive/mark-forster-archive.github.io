AF - Simplicity on the far side . . .

  • So this is day 4 of AF for me - 3 observations so far . . .

    1) Intuitive vs Rational - I have always been a more "flighty, intuitive" person by nature so the way that AF gives to sort and select tasks is absolutely brilliant for me. It is putting a system in place that is not at war with my inherent leanings. So far my productivity has gone way up and the days are flying by!!

    2) "Trust the System" - This is a biggie - yesterday I had a tremendous amount of incoming work and mental stress, much of which subsided as I worked hard to let go and trust my brain to sort it out. This really is a trick of the system - can we trust our intuitive and rational minds to work in harmony to solve the problems or situations that arise?

    3) Simplicity - The set up is minimal and the maintenance unbelievably smooth - This is a key for folks like me for whom complexity bogs down - Love it!!

    I have always resonated with the quote " I wouldn't give a fig for the simplicity on this side of complexity; I would give my right arm for the simplicity on the far side of complexity." I believe that AF is a great embodiment of that - Thank you Mark for deconstructing success and giving us a beautifully simple tool.

    I continue the experiment with interest. . .
