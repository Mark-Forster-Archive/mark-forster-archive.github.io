Lots to do with Toodledo

  • I've been away from the forum for a bit, now trying to get back on track with tasks, stuff, life etc.

    A week ago I got my first 'proper' smartphone here in the UK. It's a Samsung Galaxy SII sim-free (yes, expensive, but I need to change sim-cards according to which country I'm working in).

    I've been very impressed with Android and the screen size 4.27" is ideal for moi's eyes and even chunky fingers. So far I've been able to resist downloading lots of apps, and these are the ones that I have downloaded in case anyone else is interested:

    Skype Mobile - great, no longer have to be tied to my notebook computer, at least until Microsnot mess it up.
    My Tracks - records walks, cycling whatever
    Barcode scanner
    Google Goggles - may replace above app after testing
    Evernote - nuff said, bye-bye OneNote
    WhatsApp - clever (scary?) app which enables free texting, no more SMS, group chats to keep all members updated and in sync (eg for going away for a weekend, times, who brings what etc), a bit like having SMS always on.

    To-Do List - this is a trial version for another few days, not perfect, HOWEVER it syncs both ways with Toodledo Pro on my notebook computer (Toshiba w/Windows 7), no need to wait forever for Toodledo to bring out an Android version.

    Back to my tasks, with or without paper or smartphone.
