    Apple TV is a digital media player, it is a small network appliance designed to play digital content from the iTunes Stone, Netflix, YouTube, iCloud, etc.

    Supported formats:

    Video:

    H.264 up to 720p at 30 frames per second (1st & 2nd generation)
    H.264 up to 1080p at 30 frames per second (3rd generation)
    M4V
    MP4
    MOV
    MPEG-4
    Motion JPEG

    Audio:

    HE-ACC (V1)
    ACC
    MP3
    AIFF
    WAV
    Dolby Digital (5.1 surround sound pass through) (aka AC-3)
