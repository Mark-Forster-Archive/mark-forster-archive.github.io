  • Whichever you choose. Give it time and try to follow the rules as closely as possible. If you try to remake AF into what you want it to be, you will probably just end up with a to-do list.

    After some time and practice with any of the systems you can easily take the skills learned (allowing items to stand out) to other versions.

    Not that it matters much to you, but I practiced AF1 for some time then AF2 and found that depending on my situation I use one or the other. It is pretty clear to me when each is more appropriate.
