    It's actually a contour map of the traditional site of Mount Sinai, where according to the Bible God gave the 10 Commandments to Moses, with the area immediately around it. The low plain to the left of the Mount is where the episode of the Golden Calf took place. St Catherine's monastery is in the valley running above the Mount.

    It's a bit difficult to follow because it's tiled and repeated several times, but if you look it up on Google Earth you'll be able to work it out.
