DWM with the 13 Month Calendar

  • Ok, I have no idea how many of you are aware about the 13 month calendar so here is a explanation to put everyone on par.

    The fact that some months in our Gregorian calendar are longer then others is no news to anyone. The reason for this is that some people like Julius Caesar and Augustus Caesar thought themselves important enough to have their own months like all the roman gods but really a bit more important to not get 30 days but 31.

    Now all those days where removed from the last month of the year which at that time was february thus we have a 28 day month!

    Some efforts at standardization have been made but for some reason or another, none have sticked. One of those is the "13 Month Calendar", the "International Perpetual Calendar" or the "International Fixed Calendar" which is made of 13 months of 4 weeks each of 7 days each + 1 year day: 365 days. In leap years, you get a leap day inserted in summer.

    What this gives you is months that always have 4 weeks, always start on a sunday and end on a saturday, the 11th of each month is ALWAYS a wednesday for example and all sorts of other benefits plus weird coincidences such as each week could actually be represented by a playing card!!!

    Anyways, since DWM does not need exact dates and some people already use it dateless for days they do not work on their list, why not adopt a 28 days month scheme...?
