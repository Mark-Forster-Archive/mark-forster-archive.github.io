Autofocus Application

  • Hello all

    We have created a simple Autofocus application for Windows. You are welcome to visit its home page:

    http://www.autofocusapplication.com/

    Autofocus application is basically an electronic notebook which can be used instead of the paper notebook. It's very simple to use; I believe you won't need any instructions at all. However, if anyone wish to read a short description of its features and usage, we've created a brief instruction document here:

    http://www.autofocusapplication.com/documentation/brief-instructions.html

    I'd love to hear your thoughts about it. We'll develop it further and support other AF systems as well, so your suggestions for improvements and new features are welcome.
