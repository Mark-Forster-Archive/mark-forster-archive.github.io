  • For this I maintain a Waiting For list (one thing I've continued with from GTD days).

    I put any item that's waiting for a response from someone else on this list (including any deliveries I'm expecting). I record the date I enter the item, the person/company name, a description, optionally a target delivery date (for the cases where someone has committed to a date) and finally a follow up date.

    I have a recurring task on my AF list to 'Check Waiting For'. When I hit this task a quick scan of my W/F list picks up any task that is due a follow up, once I've worked on it I put in a new follow up date. When I've finished my W/F List scan I cross out the AF entry and re-enter it on the Active part of the list.

    Personally I find it helpful to keep all these items together rather than spread through my AF list.
