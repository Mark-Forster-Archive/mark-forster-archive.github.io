Joy/excitement is the next thing to do

  • As Mark has said, organizing by importance or urgency undermines the intuition (inner-tuition). Excitement I view as e-motion (energy in motion) flowing as expressing our true self and is therefore the criterion for the best next thing to do and is my preferred organizing principle for trusting what is the next thing to take action on that I can act on at the time. This is the engine behind autofocus in my view, it also acts to auto-intend.
