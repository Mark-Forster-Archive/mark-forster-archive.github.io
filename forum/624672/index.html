Batching / emptying inboxes

  • Hi Mark and others,

    Well I managed about 2 days without another question! I'm back at work after a bout of man flu and I'm unsure how to approach emptying my inboxes with AF.

    For example with emails, should I...

    1: Write something to the effect of 'clear emails' on my AF list - and then cross the item out and re-write it untill the task is done, at the same time taking tasks out of the emails and listing them in AF.

    2: Write 'clear emails' only once that day and cross the item out when I'm leaving the office.

    3: Schedule a time (eg first thing in the morning) to empty all of my in boxes and list related tasks into the AF list.

    Appologies if this question has already been answered - I would be happy to be re-directed to a relevant link.
    9 January 2009 at 8:34 | Leon
  • Leon:

    I think this is something about which individuals can decide what works for them best. But for the record my practice is to have a recurring item "Email" and deal with them as one batch, extracting any particularly complicated or difficult ones as separate tasks.
