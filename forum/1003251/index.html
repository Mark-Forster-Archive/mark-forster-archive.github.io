Dead, done for, dismissed, deceased

  • How to make the "dead, done for, dismissed, deceased" sting, and sting bad. I can think of only one way. When the day arrives (7 or 30 days), tasks unactioned get deleted. No someday/maybe list... no reentering by changing a pronoun or verb. The only way the deleted task/project gets re-added to the list, is when guilt, anxiety and other neurotic motivators outweigh the benefits of not doing the task/project. Hell, the aforementioned feelings are swirling around my head anyway, might as well use them to my advantage.
