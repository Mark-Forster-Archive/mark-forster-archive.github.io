How to Stop Switching Systems?

  • I switch systems every couple of days, moving from AF2 to FV to FFVP. This, of course, produces very shaky results at best. Have any of you found a way to avoid this chronic switching?
    25 February 2018 at 17:33 | PerplexedPotato
  • Make a public commitment to stick to one system and do so. After about a week, you'll find the urge disappears. Remember that you can flex the system to fit your personality and what you need while still being the system.
    25 February 2018 at 20:29 | Ryan Freckleton
  • PerplexedPotato:

    Well in fact you are only switching between two systems: AF2 and FFVP. This is because FV is within the rules of FFVP as I mention in my post at http://markforster.squarespace.com/blog/2018/1/6/change-to-fast-fvp.html (see the afternote).

    Are you using the same list when you change systems? If you are not, I strongly advise that you do. Using the same list all the time will prevent the results being patchy. In fact there is nothing really wrong with what you are doing as long as you don't keep starting new lists.
