Where to start working the DWM list?

  • Someone mentioned in a post that they always start working DWM from the point at which they'd left off, without paying special attention to the Today, Today + 7 or Today + 28 pages. This appealed to me, because I had been spending time reviewing those pages and didn’t like the jumping around. So lately I’ve been going straight to the leave-off point. Even dismissal is something I’ve been doing only when I've been round the list and got back to the top. Then I find that there are a lot of things up for the chop that I haven’t been attending to during their last days of life.

    It’s occurred to me that this would be fine for someone who gets round their list quickly. My problem is that I get through the list terribly slowly - it takes me a week to get around a list that‘s currently of 102 items. (And that doesn’t include scheduled or time-sensitive items, which go into my calendar. I dare not entrust these to the DWM list.) I don’t get to see most of the items for long stretches.

    If I add “Read through the list” as a task, it takes a week for that to come up. If I schedule the reading - oh bloody hell, that’s one more thing my calendar is nagging me to do. And I’m not going to put it into my precious Current Initiative slot at the beginning of the day.

    It seems that for sluggards like me, starting at the top of the list is best, so that’s what I’m going to be trying from now.

    I’d be interested in others’ experience.
