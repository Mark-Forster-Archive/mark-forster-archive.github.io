  • Are you asking about regular palm pilots (like a palm tx or treo) or a new palm pre? I don't have a pre, but I've used a tx for years now.

    I would think that just the regular tasks and calendar would actually work very well for DWM. It's pretty easy to assign due dates 1w or 1m out from the tasks window, and the calendar view has one which shows appts and the first several tasks due that day or in the next few days if their aren't any due that day. Also, it flags when tasks are overdue, so it would be easy to see which tasks should be reviewed and dismissed.

    I haven't tested this out, since I prefer paper for this task, and because my palm just bricked and I have no urgent need to transfer everything to my backup palm (yes, I had a second device sitting around just in case the one I was using broke.). But that's what I recall from trying to use my palm for AF4, and what worked against the palm in that scenario is in DWMs favor.

  • I have been trying DWM on my Palm TX very successfully. I use the built-in todo application and have assigned a single category to it, but that's because I need to clear out a lot of old dated todos in my other categories. I have enjoyed using very much an excellent shareware application called CanDo, ( www.elkasoft.com ) which adds features to the built-in todo lists. One useful feature for DWM is the option to change a due date to "One week from today." It also has great filtering capablity. I can easily enter items from Palm Desktop, synch my palm, and take my list with me. When I was using AF4, it worked well by having one category be the Closed List and another be the Open List.
