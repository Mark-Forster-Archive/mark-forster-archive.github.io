  • Hi Carole

    What I have done/would do in those situations is as follows:

    has already been actioned - cross it off
    you can't remember why you wanted to do it - dismiss it
    you can't understand what it was that you wrote down! - dismiss it
    if it is a task that is no longer necessary - (a) cross it off if it is just that circumstances have changed (b) dismiss it if you have taken an alternative action

    The reason I would dismiss some of those items rather than cross off, is to provide an overview of the type of tasks I am dismising - and also i may later remeber the importance etc when reviewing dismissed items.

    It is important to remember that you do not dismiss odd items before the page is finished - you dismiss all or none.

    As to the procrastination issue - if I have already done one of the items and cross it off I do treat that as my action on the page - I will have to go back to that page and make a decision next time around so do not really see that as procrastinating. Hope that helps!
