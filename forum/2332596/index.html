Integration of FV and Random

  • Here's how I've been combining FV and the Random method and it's been amazing. First, my default, "go-to" method is the random method, where I just pick a number at random and do that task. This method has been described elsewhere, and it has the advantage of completely eliminating resistance. Little and often apply, and I throw anything and everything at the list, as other autofocus systems.

    However, at the beginning of the day, and whenever necessary, I start at the end of the list and ask "is there anything I need to work on right now, before I start the random method?" Sometimes two or three tasks stand out, sometimes none. If there are tasks that stand out, I put a dot beside them and do them in whatever order feels right. Then, back in random mode I start with the last task completed.

    The idea is to be on top of my stuff enough that I don't have to resort to FV, but it's there if I need it. This way, deadlines are met and urgent matters can be addressed easily. What do you think?
