Statistics and comments after 2 months AF use

  • I've been using AF from day 1 (work only!) and am still going strong. I thought I'd make a small check after 2 full months of using the system. So I did some quick counting and came up with the following numbers, for those who like this kind of information ;)

    25 total pages
    11 first pages closed
    713 items in total
    132 open items (i.e. 18.5%)
    Two thirds of open items (88) in the last five pages
    An average of 5 open items in the other active pages
    Only 3 dismissed items

    My global assessment now is that I'm more productive than ever. Procrastination, it not totally eliminated, is strongly reduced. I'm moving forward on lots of subjects in parallel, a lot of which would have been left lying around in the past because less important. The nice thing is that the speed of movement on all these subjects is automagically adjusted depending on current priorities and urgencies.

    Some other more detailed feedback is possible, but the bottom line is: I'm not going to abandon this approach any time soon. Thanks Mark!

  • After 3 more weeks of AF (including 5 days off), the numbers look as follows:

    30 total pages
    17 pages closed
    860 items in total
    142 open items (i.e. 16.5%)
    An average of 14 open items in pages 21-30
    Only 7 dismissed items

    Another observation: after the first two months of great productivity, I experienced in the last weeks a kind of slump where I seem to start procrastinating on the whole AF list rather than on particular items. A kind of energy low which I have difficulty to attribute. Either the fact that after more than 2 months of AF the novelty effect I talked about elsewhere seems to be wearing off. Or it's just the impact of some sad event in our family life. Probably some of both...

    Anyway, the way to get out of this slump has been to... open up my list and start working the system again. And somehow I seem to be gradually getting up to speed again and rediscover the magic of AF...

  • The end of March situation (i.e. 3 full months or 53.5 working days of AF use) looks as follows:

    33 total pages
    20 first pages closed
    975 items in total (i.e. a bit more than 18 per working day)
    8 dismissed items only
    149 open items
    818 items checked off (i.e. a bit more than 15 per working day)

    The average number of new items per day (18) is slightly higher than the average number checked off (15). This was also the case after 2 months. I'm not sure yet if I need to worry about this, as it implies that my list will slowly keep growing longer... But the difference is not that large, so maybe it will even out or even reverse after I adjust some more to the system.
