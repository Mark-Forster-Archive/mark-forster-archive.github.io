Autofocus & Omnifocus / a working GTD system

  • Hi all,

    As you know i hardly struggle to find a working system. I have so many things to do and so many projects that i had a lot of difficulties to find the right thing to do at the right time.

    I finally found something wich works since 3 days and share it immediatly with you ;)) It's the basic method and the beginning of my new organisation.

    My objective was to colllect and do all my stuff in a real trusted system and be able to handlle it everywhere.
    So I chose Omnifocus. With it i can manage my projects, i can chose the date to begin things and to put dead line only when it is necessary.

    My secont objectif was to collect my stuff during the day like a Inbox and do things wich i could do in less than 2 mn or urgent things if the need to be done ASAP.
    I chose AF on paper for it capacity to follow things and intuition processing wich make me efficient on the right tasks.

    Now I explain how i deal with these tools:

    1. During the day i note on my spiral notebook every incoming information and treat them with the AF way. (read twice, report ...) and cross them as they are done. I also note info in omnifocus in the in box if i know that maybe I w'ont do it today...

    2. At night i close AF. I report all undone things in Omnifocus. With dates, comments, links... and cross items in AF.
    3. Then i do a daily review on omnifocus. Simplify every thing, eliminate unusefull stuff, cross things done, working on my projects in a GTD way. I use flag for every item wich have to be followed during my day, I use + for every item i need to do the next day. I put the name of all person concerned by my items so that i can search easely what's about what when i'll have them on the phone (i have a lot of calls)
    4. I cross every item in AF so that i will start with a blank page the next morning. I delete and clean all my emails. I report important delay or project name in ical.

    Then all my stuff in in omnifocus. All my projects are followed. All my start & end dates are Ok on omnifocus and during the day i have a + perspective (stuff to do), the search field (for people) the start perspective for everything to start at a date, and a deadline for everything which has a real deadline.

    In the morning when i arrive at my desk i put on paper all my mind items. As i sleept my spirit in creative and goes straight to the point. I put of my brain all stuff wich curse me.
    then i make my routine task again treat emails, have a look on my my appointments, read my contexts in omnifocus...

    Then i begin my day and open omnifocus.
    I read all my context wich are classified by projects add + and flags if necessary
    report on AF what must absolutly be done.
    Cross what needs to be

    Then i have on my desk My autofocus spiral notebook with a clean list all my intuition items reported (ideas from the past night) and all my rationnal tasks reported from Omnifocus.

    During the day i treat my AF and context list wich remains open on my computer in the AF way (read twice) rename in OF, report in AF and cross both little by little.
    If i have to have a look on my project i go on OF all my project is there, plan and clean with all my document linked.

    And when i have finished i just cross all my unusefull items on AF before reporting them on Omnifocus

    I can tell you folks it's a bomb. I can now follow my stuff, take it on appointments and work everywhere with my iphone and omnifocus, sync it with mobileme and do the right thing at the right time.

    What is amusing is that in fact since i do this i never missed an important or urgent item and it bluff my clients when i have them on call and report in 1 mn all the stuff and projects we have together, (just because i put their name in AF ar the right item).

    Hope this will help.
