  • Sorry I was not clear enought so I explain again. Forget about close or open list.

    Imagine you are on a AF4 list. A usual one.
    At the end of your list you have a line
    Here begins your new tasks and before you have your old one.

    Now imagine your part the all page with a vertical line, just in the middle of the page.

    So on your left colomns are your tasks old and new (after the horizonal line) and on the right a colum where you could put nothing but your urgent tasks.

    With this method you could focus on the right column to see your urgent task.
    On upper right part you have your old urgent tasks undone, on the bottom after the line you have all your new urgent tasks.
