  • I was trying out Seraphim's method of having two fixed contexts (like work and home) on the same page but didn't work out so well. Many C2 tasks I couldn't attempt due to the constraints of the context I was in had to be cancelled and re-entered. I was afraid I would lose the feeling of urgency in completing C2 tasks this way, so I abandoned the trial.

    I am now trying out the interleaving method of having personal and work tasks on alternating pages. It is working better.

    Not entirely addressing the issue you raised David, but I was thinking that for a person who travels a lot, it might be worthwhile to have "travel" pages opened each time you go on a trip and circulate around them while away. Or pick things from the other contexts and enter them to the travel pages if they can be done.
