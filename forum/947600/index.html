  • Laurence,

    As you indicated, AF4 developed organically as a result of the collaboration of the users here. As we worked with it, we found various versions inadequate for our needs and so the next version was developed by Mark. That there has been no further development lately is probably due to the robustness of the full set of AF versions. AF4 is the best for most, but some might be happier with the way previous versions work for them. Most of us use AF in conjunction with other tools and in our own particular ways. I suspect that there is no reason for anything further, other than the tweaks each of us make on our own.

    As it is now, the forum serves to answer questions from new AF user and discuss other time management topics, as well as various off-topic things that occur in any social group. I.E. it's a warm and friendly place with familiar faces ;-)
