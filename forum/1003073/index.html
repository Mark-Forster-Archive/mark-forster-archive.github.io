Urgent and Recurring Items

  • Some people are worried about putting urgent or frequently recurring items on the list at the 1-month and then 1-week points. It's important to realise that the dates in the diary do not refer to the date something should be actioned but are a means of purging tasks which have not been dealt with.

    Since all the diary pages are treated as one long list, all parts of the list are able to be accessed at any one time. So an urgent item is just as likely to get done on the page for Mar 2 as on the page for Feb 2.

    As an illustration, I actioned the task "Comments" twenty-one times over the past three days, and each time was in accordance with the rules.

    And my current task "Write comment on urgent tasks" was put on the Mar 2 page by me earlier this morning.

    The next task on the Mar 2 page? "Write comment on US/UK use of "calendar" etc."

    I think there are three key points that should be hammered into our heads:

    (1) The active list starts at today's date and ends where you've written any new tasks that occur to you today (on the page with the same date of the following month).

    (2) You work the active list in the same way as previously in Autofocus, checking each task to see if it stands out as ready to be worked on. This means that every single task on the list is available to be worked on right this instant, whether it appears on the page with today's date, or on the page with a date one month from today, or on any page in between. So an urgent task which just appeared today, and needs to be worked on today, can be actioned by moving through the list ignoring all the items before it. Once it's been actioned, you carry cycling through each item on the list again. Some peoplle may only cycle through the list once every few days, but most people will have urgent tasks on their list, and may cycle through the list many times a day.

    (3) When you open your notebook at today's date, the list of items will be composed of:

    (a) crossed-off tasks which were added either 1 month ago (and have been crossed off because they were worked on since they were added), or 1 week ago (and have been crossed off because they were worked on since they were added)
    (b) open tasks which were added 1 month ago (and have not been worked on since, so if you don't work on them today they get dismissed),
    (c) open tasks which were added 1 week ago (and have not been worked on since, so if you don't work on them today they get dismissed).
