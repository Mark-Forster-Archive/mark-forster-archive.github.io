Undated planners and long lists?

  • I'm thinking of using an undated planner with a large number of blank-ish pages as a combined "diary/schedule" and long list system. Has anyone tried this before? The idea is that the monthly calendar view would let me put reminders, schedule meetings, and do other "planner" type stuff, but then the large number of undated pages would allow me to simply use that space as a giant long list with dates at the top indicating the day that page was started.

    The idea is to keep a paper diary and the long list in the same, single book as opposed to keeping two separate books. Anyone tried this?
