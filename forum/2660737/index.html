  • I started with Do it tomorrow. Bought the book in 2010, because of good reviews on amazon.co.uk, but didn't read it until 2012. Thought Do it tomorrow was fabulous, but after few days I found the YouTube video with Autofokus and also found this site. Got a bit confused, but switched to Autofokus and it was even more fun working. After just a few weeks with Autofocus, Final version was released. I didn’t want to switch. I was so happy with Autofokus, but it was impossible not to try Final version. So I switched and stayed with Final version for a long time. I have learned that switching to a new system or switch to different tweaks of a system, is not a problem, but it makes my work more fun! Together with the lists I have used random.org, selecting 6 tasks and throwing a dice, used time boxing and Pomodoro.
    I also find it very interesting to read the discussion on what is reducing resistance and how to find the right balance between different types of tasks with a system.
    I have not tested all of the systems. Now I use “the Bounce” and I have used it for only a week. It is really fun and I get a lot done. I have not used all of the systems, but now the Bounce is my favorite.
