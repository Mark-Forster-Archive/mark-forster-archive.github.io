  • Erin -

    My Omnifocus setup is loosely based on ideas from DWM, GTD and 7 Habits. The gist:

    1. Folders that represent each of my Roles & Sharpen the Saw items
    2. Projects (these receive a due date of 7 days - subsequently all tasks associated with this project receive the same due date)
    3. Next actions for each project

    All reoccurring and new projects are given a 1 Week due date. Unlike DWM, I employ GTD's Someday idea via the "On Hold" feature in Omnifocus. However if I'm such a lazy slob, and can't even manage to change a project status to "On Hold", and it is overdue, I dismiss (delete) the project from Omnifocus.

    I also make extensive use of the notes field (in projects) and provide updates of thinking/feeling/progress/struggles for every project - whenever I work on it, and during my weekly review.
