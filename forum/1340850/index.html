  • Jupiter: give AF2 a try if you are in the mood for testing things out. You might be surprised how well it deals with the urgent stuff at the end of the list, whilst pulling you towards the block dismissal points at the beginning. And dismissal is automatically applied everyday, thus forcing you to think about those niggling tasks that simply won't stand out.

    This might put those things in the middle at a bit of a risk of being neglected, but as soon as that realisation hits, you tend to pay attention to them after the urgent stuff is handled.
