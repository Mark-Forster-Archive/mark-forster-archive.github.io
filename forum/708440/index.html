Only 3 active pages is boring

  • I finally got down to 3 active pages, and I have to admit, it's kind of boring. (Technically only 2 pages since the last item on the oldest page is to pay my son for the month of March - hmmm I could dismiss it....) :-) OTOH, 22 active pages just a week or so ago wasn't incredibly fun either, but I felt a surge of energy in wanting to bring down the numbers. Somewhere there's a happy medium, and I'm sure I'll find it. I promised myself that once I got below 5 pages, I'd go through the dismissed items from my earlier dumping/dismissing sessions, which I have, and there's nothing I'm prepared to bring back in just yet.

    Once the next hard week at work is over, I'm planning to bring 2-3 of my renovation projects onto the list (one easy, one harder). I'm not sure where to start though as by this time I had initially planned to spend April-May working outside on the yard, deck, garage etc., but the weather isn't cooperating with me. Whatever happened to global warming??

    I miss the variety that I had with my old, longer list. Yet I don't want to get to the point again of dumping onto the list and dismissing later. I've created a void of sorts, and need to fill it with something other than cleaning and renovating. Finally, I have time to pursue a hobby that works with discretionary time! At least until the weather cooperates and I can go hiking and play tennis again.
