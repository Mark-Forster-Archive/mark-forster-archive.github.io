Dismissal with AF4

  • In Marks AF4 description he mentions that, when no item in the closedlist/backlog stands out they are ready for dismissal. If the items are not ready to re-enter or to delete, Mark suggests to: "it into a reminder system for review at a later date."

    Why can't the items stay in the AF-list for a later review like in AF1?

    26 January 2010 at 23:43 | Simon W
  • The forced-consideration of dismissed items is one of the things I like best about AF4.

    When I make that first pass through the highlighted items, I make a decision about them then and there. There are three things that can happen to dismissed items:
    1) they get re-entered. In this case I follow the entry with an (r) just to remind myself that it has already been dismissed once.
    2) they get entered either as dated reminders in my calendar for a time when I think I will be able to work on them OR transferred to a project list (I have lists for sewing/craft projects, possible topics for blog entries, home improvement projects, decluttering projects, etc.)
    3) they get deleted. deleted items DO occasionally get back into the list, but when they do it's because they've occurred to me again not because of any external reminder.
