What happens when the notebook runs out?

  • I realize no one has gotten to this point yet, but I was wondering if anyone had given it some thought:

    What happens in DWM when you get to the last month/last ~30 pages in your planner?

    The easy solution is to use a loose-leaf notebook/planner, but if you're not, it seems that you might have to carry two books with you, or find some way of transferring pages from the old to the new.

  • I ran out of room / my notebook finally fell apart (I'd been using the same one since AF1 and it was almost entirely dependent on lots of duck tape to stay together) about 10 days into DWM. Here's what I did:

    Copied over all active tasks into the new notebook. For lists that were already closed (i.e., "today" through day 6) I put multiple days per page, with a line to show where days ended. For days 7 through 29 (where they were still open) I gave each day 1 side of a sheet of paper. For days 30 and on, I gave each day both sides of a sheet of paper.

    I don't know what I'll do when I have to transition to the NEXT notebook (this one only has 80 sheets, so it won't be all *that* long). They're pretty small (I bought a bunch on clearance) so maybe I'll just carry two for a while.
