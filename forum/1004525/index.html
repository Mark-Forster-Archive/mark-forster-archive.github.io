  • Acedia - I put things that can't be started yet on my calendar on the first day it can be started, at 8am (0 min duration) as a tickler. On that day I move it to my AF/DWIM list and work on it as appropriate.

    I do the same thing for recurring things that are once a week or less often - seeing it on the AF/DWM lists every day is just too much "mental chatter" for me. If I can't do until next Tuesday, I don't want to see it at all UNTIL next Tuesday - especially since there's a really good chance that on Tuesday, I'd skip over it on the list, again, because it's 'melted into the background'.
